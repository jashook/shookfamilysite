John Richard Shook J.R. Texas Atascosa John Richard Shook

John Richard Shook immigrated from Illinois to Texas sometime between 1850 and 1860. In the 1850 census, John was a school teacher. He enlisted in the Confederate army in 1862, and by 1870 had a family and was practicing law. From census records, John settled in the San Antonio area where he practiced law (as did his son Clive and brother-in-law Augustus). John is buried in City Cemetery #5 in San Antonio, Bexar County, Texas.
