Notes

Texas became a state in 1845. So 1850 was the first time Texas was included in the United States census.

Daniel, Jefferson, Josiah, and Nathan were the only Shook families in Texas in 1850. All four families were related. Daniel, Jefferson, and Nathan were brothers and descendents of Jacob Shook. Josiah was the descendent of Abraham Shook. Both Jacob and Abraham were descendents of Johanne Jacob Shook.

Harris (Josiah), Liberty (Jefferson), and Walker (Nathan) counties are all in Southeast Texas. Lamar (Daniel) county is in Northeast Texas bordering Arkansas (where Daniel lived prior to Texas).
