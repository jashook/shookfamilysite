Notes

Johannes history is recorded very well in Wilma Hicks Simpson's novel Greater Than The Mountain Was He.

Immigration: October 17, 1732, Arrived in Philadelphia on the Ship "John & William" via Rotterdam.

Johannes settled in the Williams Township area of Northampton County, Pennsylvania and remained there until his death. He and Anna Marie were members of the Old Williams Township Evangelical Lutheran Church (or Saint Luke's near Hellertown, Northampton County, Pennsylvania)
